Adult ADHD Screener

Experts believe that 80 percent of adults with ADHD are undiagnosed.

One of the major reasons is that most adults do not know what behaviors are actually symptoms. This screener is not intended as a diagnostic tool. Rather it is intended to help you start a conversation with a healthcare provider about the possibility for ADHD evaluation.

Use the slider to rate behavior frequency on a scale of 1 to 5. 1 being never. 2 being rarely. 3 being sometimes. 4 being often. 5 being very often.
